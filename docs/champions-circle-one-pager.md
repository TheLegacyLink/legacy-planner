# Legacy Link Champions Circle (One-Page Team Sheet)

## Program Mission
Legacy Link Champions Circle rewards what matters most:
1. Production
2. Sponsorship
3. Community Impact

## Legacy Credits
- **1 credit** for every **$1 Net Placed AP**
- **4,000 credits** for every **Sponsorship Policy Placed + Paid**
- **500 credits** for every **Verified Community Service Hour**
- **Service credit cap:** max 4 hours/month counted for credits (2,000 credits max/month)

> Additional verified hours beyond 4/month still count toward Community Impact bonus levels and recognition.

## Monthly Cash Bonuses
### Production
- $10,000 AP = $500
- $20,000 AP = $1,250
- $35,000 AP = $2,500
- $50,000 AP = $4,000
- $75,000 AP = $7,500

### Sponsorship
- 5 policies = $1,000
- 10 policies = $3,000
- 15 policies = $5,000

### Community Impact
- 4 hours = recognition + merch
- 8 hours = $250 bonus or marketing credit
- 12 hours = $500 bonus + spotlight recognition

## Quarterly All-Around Bonus
- **Bronze:** $40,000 AP + 3 sponsorship policies + 6 service hours = **$1,500**
- **Silver:** $75,000 AP + 5 sponsorship policies + 10 service hours = **$3,500**
- **Gold:** $120,000 AP + 8 sponsorship policies + 12 service hours = **$7,500**

## Annual Trip: Legacy Leadership Retreat
### Base Qualifier
- 150,000 Legacy Credits
- Min $100,000 personal production
- Min 12 service hours
- Good standing

### Elite Qualifier
- 225,000 Legacy Credits
- Min $150,000 personal production
- Min 8 sponsorship policies
- Min 24 service hours
- Includes room upgrade

### Legacy Architect
- 300,000 Legacy Credits
- Min $200,000 personal production
- Min 12 sponsorship policies
- Min 36 service hours
- Includes upgraded experience + guest + VIP dinner + stage recognition

## Quick Rules
- Only Net Placed AP counts
- Sponsorship counts only when placed + paid
- Community hours must be verified
- Good standing required
- Lapses/chargebacks/rescinds can reduce totals
- Highest tier per category is paid monthly
- Categories stack (Production + Sponsorship + Community + Quarterly)
