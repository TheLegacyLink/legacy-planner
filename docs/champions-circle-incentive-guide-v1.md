# Legacy Link Champions Circle — Official Incentive Guide (V1)

## Legacy Credits
- 1 credit per $1 Net Placed AP
- 4,000 credits per sponsorship policy (placed + paid)
- 500 credits per verified community service hour
- Service credit cap: 4 hours/month for credit purposes

## Monthly Bonuses
### Production
- $10,000 AP = $500
- $20,000 AP = $1,250
- $35,000 AP = $2,500
- $50,000 AP = $4,000
- $75,000 AP = $7,500

### Sponsorship
- 5 policies = $1,000
- 10 policies = $3,000
- 15 policies = $5,000

### Community Impact
- 4 hours = recognition + merch
- 8 hours = $250 bonus or marketing credit
- 12 hours = $500 bonus + spotlight recognition

## Quarterly All-Around
- Bronze: $40,000 AP + 3 sponsorship + 6 service = $1,500
- Silver: $75,000 AP + 5 sponsorship + 10 service = $3,500
- Gold: $120,000 AP + 8 sponsorship + 12 service = $7,500

## Data Integrity
- Single source of truth statuses: submitted -> approved/placed -> paid -> reversed
- Credits only from qualifying statuses
- IDs required: internal + carrier-specific where available
- Chargebacks and rescissions trigger reversals
- Bonus-hit alerts are emailed when thresholds are first crossed per period
