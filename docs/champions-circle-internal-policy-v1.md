# Legacy Link Champions Circle — Internal Policy (V1)

**Owner:** Legacy Link Leadership + Operations  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** [Insert Date]

## 1) Purpose
Legacy Link Champions Circle is a performance incentive system designed to reward three pillars:
- Production
- Sponsorship
- Community Impact

## 2) Eligibility
An agent is eligible when they are:
- Active
- In good standing
- Properly attributed in system-of-record data
- Verified where required (community service documentation)

Leadership and Operations retain final payout authority.

## 3) Definitions
- **Net Placed AP:** Issued/active premium net of reversals
- **Sponsorship Policy Placed + Paid:** Sponsorship-attributed policy with paid status
- **Verified Community Service Hour:** Logged + validated service hour
- **Legacy Credits:** Program points for ranking and annual qualifiers

## 4) Credit Formula
- 1 credit per $1 Net Placed AP
- 4,000 credits per Sponsorship Policy (Placed + Paid)
- 500 credits per verified service hour
- Service credit cap = 4 hrs/month (2,000 monthly credits)

Hours above 4/month still count for community bonus tiers and recognition.

## 5) Monthly Bonus Structure
### 5.1 Production
- $10,000 AP = $500
- $20,000 AP = $1,250
- $35,000 AP = $2,500
- $50,000 AP = $4,000
- $75,000 AP = $7,500

### 5.2 Sponsorship
- 5 policies = $1,000
- 10 policies = $3,000
- 15 policies = $5,000

### 5.3 Community
- 4 hours = recognition + merch
- 8 hours = $250 bonus or marketing credit
- 12 hours = $500 bonus + spotlight

## 6) Quarterly All-Around Bonus
- Bronze: $40,000 AP + 3 sponsorship + 6 service = $1,500
- Silver: $75,000 AP + 5 sponsorship + 10 service = $3,500
- Gold: $120,000 AP + 8 sponsorship + 12 service = $7,500

## 7) Annual Trip Qualification
Trip: **Legacy Leadership Retreat**

- **Base:** 150,000 credits + minimum standards
- **Elite:** 225,000 credits + enhanced standards + room upgrade
- **Legacy Architect:** 300,000 credits + top standards + VIP package

## 8) Attribution and Data Integrity (Critical)
All production and sponsorship records must be tied to immutable identifiers where available:
- `agent_id` (writer)
- `sponsoring_agent_id` (sponsor)
- carrier case ID / policy ID

Name-only matching is fallback only. Disputed attribution is resolved by Operations based on source-of-truth records.

## 9) Adjustments and Reversals
Lapses, chargebacks, rescissions, compliance reversals, or fraud flags can reduce prior credits and payouts.

## 10) Payout Governance
- Calculations run post close
- Payouts released after verification window
- Highest tier per category only
- Categories can stack

## 11) Funding Framework
Legacy Incentive Pool target:
- 3–5% of backend/override revenue (or fixed budget while scaling)

Recommended split:
- 60% cash bonuses
- 20% annual trip fund
- 10% recognition/perks
- 10% community fund

## 12) Recognition and Awards
Annual award categories:
- Producer of the Year
- Sponsorship Leader of the Year
- Community Impact Leader of the Year
- Rookie of the Year
- Legacy Architect Award
- Builder of the Year
