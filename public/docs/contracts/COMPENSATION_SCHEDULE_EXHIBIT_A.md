# Exhibit A — Compensation Schedule (JumpStart)

## General
Payout eligibility requires:
1. Signed ICA on file
2. Required licensing/compliance status
3. Contracting/program gate compliance
4. Issued/approved policy conditions met

## JumpStart payout model
### Small Policy (Total $1,200)
- Special Agent: $600
- Regional Director: $300
- Legacy Visionary: $200
- Agency Owner: $100

### Large Policy (Total $2,400)
- Special Agent: $1,200
- Regional Director: $600
- Legacy Visionary: $400
- Agency Owner: $200

## Unlicensed handling
If closing/assigned agent is unlicensed:
- 50% released initially
- 50% held until licensing completion + first policy written

## No double payout
If a tier is skipped, next upline receives only their own tier amount.

## Chargebacks
Policy lapses/cancellations cause proportional clawback from relevant paid parties.
Agency may deduct from future commissions.

## Payout timing
Default payout cycle: Friday, subject to carrier/compliance timing and holdbacks.

## Change control
This schedule may be updated by written Agency notice.
