# Licensed Agent Onboarding Playbook

## Purpose
- This is your Licensed Agent onboarding checklist.
- Move fast, execute in order, and complete every requirement the same day when possible.

## Step 1 — Access Your SOP Portal
- Open your SOP link from the welcome email.
- Login with the email and password provided in your approval email.
- Complete each checklist item and submit approval requests where required.

## Step 2 — Complete Contracting (Required)
- Use the contracting link in your email to complete your profile and required setup.
- Double-check all profile fields before submitting.
- If contracting blocks you, notify support immediately with a screenshot.

## Step 3 — Community + Training
- Join Skool and confirm your membership inside SOP.
- Watch "Whatever It Takes" and complete the required action.
- Stay active and responsive in the group.

## Step 4 — Execution Standards
- Follow scripts exactly until your first production cycle is complete.
- Keep all applicant notes clean in CRM.
- Submit updates daily and maintain follow-up discipline.
