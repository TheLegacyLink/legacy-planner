5
Answer the Suitability Questions
Part 3 of 3 — Be Honest

Answer 5 simple questions. There are no wrong answers — this just helps determine if a company-funded life insurance policy is appropriate for you.

1. Do you currently have any life insurance coverage?
2. Does anyone rely on you financially? (spouse, children, family, business partner)
3. Do you have financial obligations that could create hardship if something happened to you?
4. Would your family experience financial hardship if you passed away unexpectedly?
5. Do you understand the purpose of life insurance and how it protects people?
6

⚠️ If you're not suitable right now:

You can still fully participate in Legacy Link. The policy is always optional.
