# Meeting Confirmation Templates (Approved Style)

## SMS (No full name)

Use first name only.

```text
Hi {{FirstName}} — quick reminder for your meeting today.

{{TimeLocal}}
Zoom: {{ZoomLink}}

If you need to reschedule, reply here.
```

## Email (No full name)

**Subject:** `Meeting Reminder — {{MeetingDate}} {{TimeLabel}}`

```text
Hi {{FirstName}},

Quick reminder for your meeting today.

Time:
{{MeetingDate}}
{{TimeLabel}}

Zoom: {{ZoomLink}}
Add to Calendar: {{CalendarLink}}

If you need to reschedule, just reply to this email.

- The Legacy Link Team
```

## Rules

- Never use full name in greeting/body.
- Keep spacing clean (short lines + line breaks).
- Keep CTA simple: "reply to reschedule."
